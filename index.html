Why This Opportunity Is Different

I've tried many side hustles before, from selling products online to virtual assistant work. Here's why this typing opportunity has been the most sustainable for me as a single parent:

  • Truly Flexible Hours: I work when my kids are in school or asleep. No more calling in sick when a child has a fever.
  • Complete Location Freedom: All I need is my laptop and internet connection. We've even taken "workcations" where I work a few hours in the morning and spend afternoons with my kids.
  • Stable, Predictable Income: Unlike freelancing with unpredictable client work, this provides consistent income I can count on.
  • No Selling or Marketing Required: I don't have to convince anyone to buy anything or build a personal brand.
  • Weekly Payments: No waiting 30-60 days to get paid like with many freelance gigs.

The most surprising benefit has been how this work has allowed me to be more present with my children. I'm no longer constantly stressed about money or working late shifts that took me away from them.
